Features

Built to catch the drop, not the noise.

Fare “deals” are everywhere; real ones are rare. Vigía tracks price history per route so it only raises an alert when a fare is genuinely below its usual level.

01

Continuous monitoring

Scheduled scans of your configured routes and date windows, around the clock, from a lightweight self-hosted service.

02

Baseline-aware alerts

Every fare is compared against its route’s historical baseline. Alerts fire on real drops, with the discount quantified.

03

Instant Telegram delivery

Deals land in your Telegram the moment they’re detected — with route, dates, price and deep link to book.

04

Fully configurable

Destinations, trip lengths, date horizons, price thresholds and alert cadence — all tuned from a simple config, hot-reloadable.

05

Bot-controlled operations

Pause, resume and adjust the radar directly from Telegram. No SSH required for day-to-day control.

06

Self-hosted & private

Runs in a single Docker container on your own machine. Your searches and history stay with you.

How it works

Three steps between a price drop and your pocket.

A simple pipeline, running on repeat.

1

Scan

Vigía queries current fares for every configured route and date window departing Alicante (ALC).

2

Compare

Each fare is checked against the route’s stored price history to separate genuine drops from ordinary fluctuation.

3

Alert

Qualifying deals are pushed to Telegram instantly, with the drop versus baseline quantified in every alert.
